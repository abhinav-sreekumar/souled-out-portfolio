About
NOT YOUR AVERAGE SOUND.

Two-piece R&B. Funky basslines, vocal harmonies,
chord progressions that refuse the obvious path.
Every note carries soul by design, not by accident.

The Vision

Music coming out of Chennai — and every other corner of India — has long been measured against a ceiling that was never ours to accept. Souled Out exists to dismantle that quietly. Not by chasing global trends, but by setting them. The goal is simple: music so good, its origin is the last thing anyone thinks about. And the first thing they feel is the soul.

The Artists

Abhinav
portrait photo

Guitar · Backing Vocals · Creative Direction

Abhinav
Sreekumar

Abhinav picked up the guitar in the second grade and never looked for a reason to put it down. Shaped by the funk vocabulary of Cory Wong, the groove sensibility of Parcels, and the melodic instinct of John Mayer, his playing sits at the intersection of feel and precision. Off the instrument, he works as a technologist and early adopter — drawn to artists like Daniel Rozin who collapse the boundary between code and human expression. That same philosophy bleeds into his approach to production: modern, intentional, alive.

Siddharth
portrait photo

Guitar · Lead Vocals · Lead Producer

Siddharth
Nayanar

Siddharth picked up the guitar at 15 and hasn't stopped since — or so the story goes. What followed was over 200 live performances, collaborations with legends like Arivu and Ilayaraja, and a reputation for knowing exactly how to hold a room. His production is built around precision and groove — particularly the kind of bassline that makes the decision for you before your brain catches up. When he's not in the studio, he's in the kitchen or with Kamali. Both, he'll tell you, require the same attention to detail.

Music

Cutting edge production. Funky basslines. Vocal harmonies. Not-so-pop chord progressions. Every note, a bit of soul.
